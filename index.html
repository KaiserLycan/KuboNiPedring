Frequently Asked Questions

What facilities are available at the resort?
Kubo ni Pedring, nestled in the heart of Barangay Tandang Kutyo, Tanay, Rizal, offers a peaceful mountain retreat with amenities such as a swimming pool, air-conditioned rooms, a campsite, and event spaces.
Is there free Wi-Fi at the resort?
Currently, we do not offer Wi-Fi at the resort, but we have a variety of amenities to ensure you have a fun and relaxing stay.
What activities are available at the resort?
At Kubo ni Pedring, we offer a variety of activities including a swimming pool, campsite, billiards room, and karaoke.
How do I get to the resort?
Getting to the resort can be a bit tricky due to the roads, but we provide assistance and guidance to our guests.See Travel Guide
