LEARN MORE ABOUT US:

Kubo ni Pedring, originally created as a camping site, was eventually developed as both a camping site, and with recent construction of more housing space, a private farm resort, was recently established two years ago, in 2023.

The more than one (1) hectare property it encompasses is surrounded by a scenic view of mountains and the vast green sceneries of Tanay, Rizal. It officially opened and became operational on June 01, 2024. The facility is intended to cater families or groups who would like to escape the city and enjoy the quiet and fresh air offered by nature.

Kubo ni Pedring is located around the heart of the mountains of Barangay Tandang Kutyo, Tanay, Rizal and is just an hour and a half away from Manila. Apart from family and group vacations, the facility also offers enough space suitable for events such as weddings, birthdays, team buildings, etc.
